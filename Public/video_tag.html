<video width="500" height="500" autoplay loop id="video"> <source src="/Video/RRNGGYU.mp4" type="video/mp4"> </video>

This code allows users to put a video on the webpage using video tags. The actual tag is <video> and "src" lets creators indicate the location of the video.

"Width" and "Height" dictate the size of the video, while "autoplay" and "loop" specify how the video will act on the page.
